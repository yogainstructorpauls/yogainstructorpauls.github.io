What is Wilma?

Wilma is the educational institution's www-connection. This Wilma license is owned by Optima

In Wilma, students can make course selections, monitor their performance, read notices and communicate with teachers.

In Wilma, teachers can mark grades and absences, update personal information, and communicate with students and guardians.

Through Wilma, guardians can follow up and resolve student absences, communicate with teachers, and read school notices.

Wilma is also used by the educational institution's staff, management and workplace supervisors.

Log in to Wilma by entering your username and password in the fields on the right. Then click Log in .
